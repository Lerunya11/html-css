Fullstack and Human Intelligence

This page introduces the concept of human intelligence and how it connects with the professional qualities of a Full-Stack Developer.

What is Intelligence?

Intelligence is the ability to acquire and apply knowledge and skills. It involves reasoning, problem-solving, learning, and adapting. Intelligence is a topic of interest in psychology, education, and artificial intelligence.

Source: Wikipedia – Intelligence

What is a Full-Stack Developer?

A Full-Stack Developer works on both the front end and back end of web applications. They are capable of handling the entire development process from database to user interface.

Source: Wikipedia – Full-Stack Development

Key Skills for Full-Stack Developers

  • HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Frontend frameworks like React or Angular
  • Backend technologies such as Node.js, Python, PHP
  • Database management (MySQL, MongoDB)
  • Version control systems (Git)
  • Soft skills and analytical thinking
